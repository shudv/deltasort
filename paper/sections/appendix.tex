Both BIS and DeltaSort perform similarly and have rapidly declining crossover thresholds as $n$ increases. This indicates that \emph{V8's memory movement cost model does not provide any asymptotic advantage for DeltaSort}. Hence, in V8, a valid hybrid strategy can be: use BIS/DeltaSort for $k \ll n$, ESM for $k \lesssim 50\%$, \texttt{Array.prototype.sort} for $k \gtrsim 50\%$.
